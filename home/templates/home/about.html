About Meow Sweden

Meow Sweden is founded me, Mamoni (aka Mjau), and it grew out of my urge to make my creative ideas real, and of course my love for cats ( Hi, my name is Mamoni, and I'm a cat-oholic) and pretty things.

The very beginning

It all really started out with me and my cat-oholic behavior of collecting various types of cat ears and other "cat-ccessouries". But since I always want to put my own touch on things I soon started to design and make my own cat ears. To me there's always an occasion when you can put on a pair of cat ears to boost your outfit, let your inner cat loose and bring some smiles to peoples faces.
